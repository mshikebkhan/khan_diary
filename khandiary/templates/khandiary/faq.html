FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Khan Diary

  • Is Khan Diary free?

    Yes! it is absolutely free.


  • Can Khan Diary see what I writes?

    No! privacy is Khan Diary's first promise. If your account is private then only you and God knows what you have written. But if your account is public then Yes! Khan Diary can see what you writes.


  • How much entries can I add?

    As diary entry means summary of whole day, so you can only add one entry per day. We recommand you to add entry after going to bed just before sleep.


  • Why am I not getting any notification on my email from Khan Diary?

    Khan Diary does not send you any notification to your email, you can check your notifications by Notifications page on Khan Diary.


  • How can I make my account public/private?

    Go to "Profile" => "Settings" => "Account" => "Account privacy setting".


  • How can I change my password?

    Go to "Profile" => "Settings" => "Account" => "Change password".


  • How can I reset my password?

    Under login form click on "Password reset" link and follow further steps.
