What is a diary entry?

It is a summary of the whole day. What special or ordinary things happened to you? You write all of this stuff there in brief.


Why should you write a diary entry?

It can help you gain control of your emotions and improve your mental health. Even if you struggle with stress, depression, or anxiety, keeping a diary can be a great idea.
